## How Events Work

Anyone in the community can propose and organize an event. You don't need special permission.

### Types of Events

- **Casual meetups:** Coffee or lunch at a cafe. No agenda, just hanging out and meeting people.
- **Coworking sessions:** Work together at a cafe or coworking space. Good for accountability and socializing.
- **Skill shares:** Short, informal presentations where someone shares what they know.
- **Workshops:** Hands-on sessions on topics like freelancing, coding, photography, language exchange, etc.
- **Social events:** Dinners, walking tours, day trips, and other ways to explore Istanbul together.

### How to Propose an Event

1. Open an issue in this repo using one of our templates
2. Fill in the details: what, when, where, and how many people
3. Share the link in the Telegram group
4. That's it!

## Tips for Organizers

- **Pick a good location.** Somewhere central with good wifi and enough space. Check our [resources repo](https://github.com/istanbul-digital-nomads/resources) for venue suggestions.
- **Give enough notice.** Post at least 3 to 5 days before the event.
- **Keep it simple.** The best events are often just "let's meet at this cafe at 3pm."
- **Follow up.** After the event, post a short recap in the Telegram group and add it to the archive.
- **Be inclusive.** Choose venues that are accessible and affordable. Not everyone has the same budget.
